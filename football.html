Football in the Heart, Vision in the Mind

Football is not just a sport — it is a culture, a community, and a lifelong passion. Growing up in Kerala, where football flows through every street and field, my connection with the game is deep and formative.

Where My Football Passion Comes From

Born and raised in Kerala, I have grown up surrounded by passionate football fans and grassroots football culture. From local matches to community tournaments, the sport has been a part of my life since childhood.

This connection is not just nostalgia — it shapes how I view teamwork, discipline, performance, and growth. Just like in engineering, football requires strategic thinking, resilience, and coordinated execution.

Vision & Aspirations

My long-term vision with football is not to play professionally, but to contribute meaningfully to its ecosystem in structured and impactful ways.

  • Explore data analytics for player performance and match trends.
  • Collaborate on platforms that support grassroots football development.
  • Work with local clubs on digital transformation and tactical insights.
  • Create content, discussions, and community engagement around football strategy.

Combining Tech with Football

Technology can open new doors in how football is studied, appreciated, and developed. My aim is to eventually build or support tools that help coaches, players, and clubs leverage insights — whether through analytics dashboards, performance tracking, or community platforms.
